Monetization concept

Charge a low price per pairing session.

Bundle a predictable number of browser-to-phone pair sessions into each plan, then charge a small overage when teams use more than their included amount.

Recommended Model

Included sessions

Each plan includes a monthly amount of successful pair sessions, such as 50, 250, or 1,000 sessions.

Low overage price

Extra successful pair sessions are billed at a small set price, such as $0.03 to $0.10 each.

Plan ladder

Higher plans lower the effective price per session by including more sessions up front.

Plan

Define what counts

Count only successful pair sessions that connect the browser to the App Clip or mobile app. Failed attempts, expired QR codes, retries, and internal tests should not be billable.

Track entitlement state

Store user plan, billing status, period start, period end, included sessions, sessions used in period, overage price, and any prepaid session credits.

Meter session creation

Let paid plans continue past the included amount, but mark overage sessions clearly before they accrue extra charges.

Connect billing

Use Stripe subscriptions with metered billing for overages, or invoice overages at the end of the billing period.

Review the numbers

Track included usage, overage usage, effective price per session, gross margin, churn, support issues, and whether users understand what a pair session means.

Decision Notes

Best first version

This model can work if "pair session" maps cleanly to real usage and costs. Start with plans that include generous sessions, then use a low overage price as a fairness valve instead of making every scan feel metered.

Watch-outs

  • Do not charge for failed or accidental sessions.
  • Explain exactly when a pair session starts and ends.
  • Show included usage and projected overage before billing.
  • Reset included sessions on a predictable monthly schedule.
  • Keep billing checks on the server, not only in the UI.
